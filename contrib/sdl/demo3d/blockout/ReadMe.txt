JEDI-SDL BlockOut Clone
-----------------------
This is a 3D Tetric game, based on the game BlockOut from a few
years ago. Very addictive!

Keys
----
Left, Right, Up, Down - Move block without rotation
Space                 - Drop Block to the bottom
A, S, D, Z, X, C      - Rotate blocks in various directions.


Your score appears in the Title Bar.

Enjoy!
